JS Calendar demo

To include this module in your site you need to add scripts with correct paths in your <head> section:

  • src="../jquery/dist/jquery.min.js"
  • src="js/calendarVariables.js"
  • src="js/buildCalendarV2.js"
  • src="js/drawCalendar.js"
  • src="js/sendData.js"
  • href="css/main.css"

You may add calenars in html by adding containers <div class="calendar__container">

    and simple calendars:
  • <div class="simple_calendar" data-year="2017">
  • <div class="simple_calendar" data-year="2018">

or add <select> form to add menu for calendars
